Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

Odon ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

When you use Odon, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and name when you sign up
  • Profile Information: Display name and profile picture (if provided via Google)
  • Calendar Data: Your availability times that you choose to share with your groups
  • Group Data: Groups you create or join, and your interactions within them

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Odon service
  • Coordinate hangouts with your friend groups
  • Send you notifications about check-ins and scheduled events
  • Improve our service and user experience

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your availability and group membership information is only shared with other members of groups you voluntarily join.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest. Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure.

Third-Party Services

Odon uses the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: For authentication (we only access your email and basic profile)
  • Supabase: For data storage and authentication
  • Google Gemini AI: For smart scheduling suggestions (no personal data is stored by AI)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Leave any group at any time
  • Opt out of notifications
